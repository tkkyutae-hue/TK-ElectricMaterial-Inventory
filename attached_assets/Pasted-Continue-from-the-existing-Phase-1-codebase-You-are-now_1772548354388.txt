## Data Integrity Requirements

Be careful with stock math.

### Rules:

* no issue/transfer should allow stock to go negative unless explicitly permitted
* receives increase balances
* issues decrease source balances
* returns increase destination balances
* transfers decrease source and increase destination
* adjustments must be logged with reason
* every stock-changing action must produce a movement record

If you maintain summary quantity on items, keep it synchronized.
