3. ATTENDANCE EVALUATION
════════════════════════════════════════

Attendance must remain a distinct evaluation category.

Use this attendance scoring system:
- ATTEND = 10
- PTO = 8
- SICK = 7
- ABSENT = 0
- OFF = excluded from calculation
- LATE = 5
- EARLY LEAVE = 4
- WFH = 8
- TRAINING = 9
- SUSPENDED = 0
- TERMINATED = excluded from calculation

Attendance score rules:
- Attendance Score = average of scored attendance records
- OFF and TERMINATED are excluded from the denominator

Attendance should appear in:
- Evaluation Summary
- Attendance Record section
- Skill Board
- Total Score calculation
- Evaluation History

Attendance interpretation:
- 9.0–10.0 = Excellent attendance
- 7.0–8.9 = Reliable
- 5.0–6.9 = Needs improvement
- 3.0–4.9 = Weak
- 0–2.9 = Critical

Attendance Record page section should include:
- monthly attendance summary
- status pill counts
- attendance score
- recent status log table

Important:
- Do NOT show the full attendance calculation formula on the page
- keep the calculation internal
- show only the result and useful summaries
- keep OFF and TERMINATED excluded
