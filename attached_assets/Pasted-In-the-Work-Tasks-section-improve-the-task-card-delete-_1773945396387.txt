In the Work Tasks section, improve the task card delete behavior.

FIX 1 — Confirm before deleting
When user clicks the trash/delete icon on a task card:
  Do NOT delete immediately.
  Show a small inline confirmation inside the card:
    "Delete this task?"  [Cancel]  [Delete]
  
  Style the confirmation as a thin bar at the bottom of the card:
    background: #fee2e2 (light red)
    border-top: 1px solid #fecaca
    padding: 8px 14px
    font-size: 11px
    color: #dc2626
    
  [Cancel] button: ghost/outline, gray
  [Delete] button: filled red (#dc2626), white text
  
  Clicking Cancel dismisses the bar — card stays.
  Clicking Delete proceeds to FIX 2 below.

FIX 2 — Undo toast after deletion
When user confirms Delete:
  1. Remove the task card from the list immediately
  2. Show a toast notification at the bottom of the screen:
     
     Text: "Task deleted"
     Action: [Undo] button on the right
     
     Toast style:
       position: fixed, bottom: 24px, left: 50%, transform: translateX(-50%)
       background: #1f2937 (dark gray)
       color: white
       padding: 10px 18px
       border-radius: 8px
       font-size: 11.5px
       display: flex, align-items: center, gap: 16px
       box-shadow: 0 4px 16px rgba(0,0,0,0.2)
       z-index: 9999
     
     [Undo] button style:
       color: #86efac (light green)
       font-weight: 700
       font-size: 11.5px
       background: none
       border: none
       cursor: pointer
     
     Auto-dismiss after 5 seconds with a subtle fade-out.
     Show a thin progress bar at the bottom of the toast
     that depletes over 5 seconds (green → empty).
     
  3. If user clicks [Undo] within 5 seconds:
     Restore the deleted task card in its original position
     with all its data intact (description, workers, status, notes, photos).
     Dismiss the toast immediately.
  
  4. After 5 seconds without Undo:
     The deletion is final — task is permanently removed.
     Toast fades out.

Do not change anything else in Work Tasks or other sections.
